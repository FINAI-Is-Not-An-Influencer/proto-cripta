## 👁️ Vision & Mission
This project rejects the definition of the web as a mere showcase for vanity. Our mission is to hack the capitalist ladder of visibility to democratize access to high-level education and semantic power. 

We aim to distribute knowledge equitably, elevate the metrics of independent researchers, and build a universal public science starting from the fundamental question of Western thought: **the problem of Being**. 

---

## 🧬 The Two Souls
This portal will eventually merge two distinct entities into a unified, interactive experience:

* **FINAI:** A Web Designer, Developer, and Marketer driven by the ethics of FLOSS (Free/Libre and Open Source Software). Focused on seizing the means of digital production and optimizing the semantic web.
* **PerversaPhilosophia (Cripta Filosofica):** A public philosophy project confronting scholars, PhDs, and scientists. A dark, speculative space full of thought, symbolized by an abandoned tomb filled with Blue Roses.

## 🛠 Purpose of this Repository
**We have not chosen our final Brand Identity yet.** This repository is strictly a **laboratory for experimentation**. It is designed to host static HTML/CSS/JS wireframes, UI components, and conceptual layouts. We are using this space to "get our hands dirty," testing different aesthetics and user experiences before committing to a final stack or brand name.

Here we will test:
* Typography and visual hierarchies.
* The balance between the "Code/Infrastructure" UI and the "Philosophical Crypt" UI.
* Dynamic toggles and user interactions.
